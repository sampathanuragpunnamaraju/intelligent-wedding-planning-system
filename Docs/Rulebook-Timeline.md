# Rulebook - Timeline

# Wedding Timeline Rulebook

## Purpose

The Timeline Rule Engine generates tasks and schedules based on the wedding date.

---

## TR-001 Planning Duration Classification

Comfortable

More than 6 Months

---

Standard

3 - 6 Months

---

Tight

1 - 3 Months

---

Critical

Less than 1 Month

---

## TR-002 Mandatory Wedding Tasks

Every wedding requires:

* Budget Planning
* Guest List Preparation
* Venue Booking
* Catering Arrangement
* Decoration Planning
* Photography Booking
* Invitation Management
* Transportation Planning
* Final Coordination

---

## TR-003 Venue Booking Timeline

Recommended

4 - 6 Months Before Wedding

---

Purpose

* Better Availability
* Better Pricing
* More Venue Choices

---

## TR-004 Catering Timeline

Recommended

2 - 4 Months Before Wedding

Tasks

* Select Caterer
* Finalize Menu
* Confirm Guest Count

---

## TR-005 Photography Timeline

Recommended

2 - 5 Months Before Wedding

Purpose

Popular photographers are booked early.

---

## TR-006 Invitation Timeline

Invitation Design

45 Days Before Wedding

---

Invitation Distribution

30 Days Before Wedding

---

## TR-007 Decoration Timeline

Theme Selection

2 Months Before Wedding

---

Final Approval

2 Weeks Before Wedding

---

## TR-008 Transportation Planning

Required When

Guest Count > 200

---

Tasks

* Airport Pickup Planning
* Bus Arrangements
* Parking Coordination

---

## TR-009 Final Week Checklist

7 Days Before

* Confirm Venue
* Confirm Caterer
* Confirm Photographer
* Confirm Decorator

---

3 Days Before

* Final Guest Count
* Final Food Count
* Final Transportation Schedule

---

1 Day Before

* Venue Inspection
* Stage Inspection
* Sound Check

---

## TR-010 Critical Alerts

Condition

Wedding Date < 30 Days

AND

Major Tasks Pending

---

Result

Critical Alert Generated

---

Example

Venue Not Booked

OR

Caterer Not Finalized

OR

Invitations Not Sent

Result

Immediate Attention Required
