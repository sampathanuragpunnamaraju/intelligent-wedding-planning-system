# Wedding Guest Rulebook

## Purpose

The Guest Rule Engine converts guest count into operational requirements and planning decisions.

---

1 - 100 Guests

---

101 - 300 Guests

---

---

Grand

601 - 1000 Guests

---

Mega

More than 1000 Guests

---

## GR-002 Venue Capacity Requirement

Formula

Required Capacity = Guest Count × 1.2

Purpose:

* Extra guests
* Staff
* Vendors
* Movement space

---

Example

Guest Count = 500

Required Capacity = 600

---

## GR-004 Parking Requirement

Formula

Parking Slots = Guest Count ÷ 4

Assumption:

1 Vehicle per 4 Guests

---

Example

Guests = 400

Required Parking = 100 Slots

---

## GR-005 Service Staff Requirement
