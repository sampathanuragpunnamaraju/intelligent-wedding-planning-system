# Rulebook - Budget

# Wedding Budget Rulebook

## Purpose

The Budget Rule Engine is responsible for:

* Budget allocation
* Cost estimation
* Budget feasibility analysis
* Budget risk detection

---

## BR-001 Budget Allocation

Default Budget Distribution

Venue = 25%

Food/Catering = 40%

Decoration = 10%

Photography = 8%

Entertainment = 5%

Transportation = 5%

Miscellaneous = 7%

---

## Formula

Category Budget = Total Budget × Percentage

---

## Example

Total Budget = ₹10,00,000

Venue Budget = ₹2,50,000

Food Budget = ₹4,00,000

Decoration Budget = ₹1,00,000

Photography Budget = ₹80,000

Entertainment Budget = ₹50,000

Transportation Budget = ₹50,000

Miscellaneous Budget = ₹70,000

---

## BR-002 Catering Cost Estimation

Simple Wedding

₹700 Per Plate

---

Standard Wedding

₹1200 Per Plate

---

Premium Wedding

₹2000 Per Plate

---

Formula

Food Cost = Guest Count × Cost Per Plate

---

Example

Guests = 500

Wedding Scale = Standard

Food Cost = 500 × 1200

Food Cost = ₹6,00,000

---

## BR-003 Food Budget Feasibility

Condition

Required Food Cost > Allocated Food Budget

---

Result

Food Budget Shortage Detected

## BR-005 Venue Budget Feasibility

Condition

Expected Venue Cost > Venue Budget Allocation

---

Result

Venue Budget Risk Detected

---

## BR-006 Emergency Buffer

Reserve

5% - 10%

of Total Budget

---

Purpose

* Unexpected Guests
* Weather Arrangements
* Transportation Changes
* Vendor Changes
* Last Minute Expenses

---

## BR-007 Overall Budget Status

Excellent

Budget comfortably supports event.

---

Good

Budget supports event with minor limitations.

---

Moderate

Budget requires optimization.

---

Poor

Budget insufficient for requirements.

---

Critical

Budget significantly below estimated requirements.
