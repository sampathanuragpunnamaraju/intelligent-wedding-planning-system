# Project Vision

# AI Wedding Planning Advisor

## Problem Statement

Planning a wedding is a complex process involving budget management, venue selection, guest management, timeline planning, and feasibility analysis.

Most existing systems focus on booking services rather than helping users understand whether their wedding plan is realistic and achievable.

## Solution

Build an AI-assisted wedding planning system that:

* Collects wedding requirements through a questionnaire
* Uses business rules to estimate costs and feasibility
* Generates wedding plans and timelines
* Provides recommendations and explanations using AI
